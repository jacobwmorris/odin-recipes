Grilled Salmon

This is the fish you always see bears cathcing as they jump upstream (very picturesque!). It can be served with salad, rice, a baked potato, or whatever else. This recipe produces 4 servings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound salmon filet, cut into 4 pieces
  • Olive oil
  • 1/4 cup garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup kosher salt
  • 1/4 cup dried parsley
  • 1/4 cup dried minced onion
  • 1/4 cup dried basil

Instructions

  1. Mix the garlic powder, kosher salt, parsley, minced onion, and basil.
  2. Rub salmon filets with olive oil, then season with the seasoning mixture.
  3. Grill on medium heat for about 5 minutes per side. Salmon is done when it flakes easily with a fork.
