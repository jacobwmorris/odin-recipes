Omelette

It's like an egg burrito. You can fill it with things like ham, peppers, mushrooms, and tomatoes. This recipe produces 1 serving.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/8 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 grinds black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon water
  • 1/2 tablespoon butter
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • Additional filling ingredients, if desired

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, then whisk together with kosher salt, pepper, and water.
  2. Melt butter in a nonstick pan just below high heat. wait until the butter starts to become foamy with large bubbles but not yet browned.
  3. Pour in eggs, and cook for 10 to 15 seconds, until a skin begins to form.
  4. Add cheese and filling ingredients. Push a spatula under the far edge of the egg, then pull up and shake the pan to spread out the remaining uncooked egg.
  5. Roll the eggs over the cheese, and cook another 10 to 15 seconds. The outside should be pale golden brown and the inside should be soft and creamy.
  6. Remove omelette from pan, folding it over itself into a rolled shape.
