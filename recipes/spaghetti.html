Spaghetti

It's basically bread, but in strings, and with sauce. This recipe makes home made sauce with ground beef. This recipe produces 8 servings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small green pepper, diced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (28 ounces)
  • 1 can tomato sauce (16 ounces)
  • 1 can tomato paste (6 ounces)
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Combine ground beef, onion, garlic, and green pepper in a large saucepan.
  2. Cook on medium-high heat until meat is browned and vegetables are tender.
  3. Drain grease.
  4. Stir in diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste.
  5. Season with oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  6. Simmer sauce for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  7. Boil pasta in a large pot.
  8. Drain pasta water and serve with sauce.
